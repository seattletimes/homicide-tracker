Homicide Tracker Map

Every year in King County, dozens of people lose their lives to acts of violence. The Seattle Times is tracking the county’s homicides in 2020, in an effort to recognize the loss and to monitor the cases as they are investigated and move through the criminal justice system. In the map and table below, you can find information about each homicide reported in King County so far this year.

Click on the case to see more details about what happened, as well as links to The Seattle Times’ coverage.
